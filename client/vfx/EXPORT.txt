Pixel-art HUD VFX (128×128 RGBA PNG, transparent), generated via PixelLab map-object API.

Regenerate:
  python3 tools/pixellab_v2.py map-object "pixel art muzzle flash … transparent" client/vfx/muzzle_flash.png
  python3 tools/pixellab_v2.py map-object "pixel art blood splatter … transparent" client/vfx/blood_overlay.png

shell_brass.png — HUD ejected brass (alpha). Regenerate: `python3 tools/gen_fps_assets.py` or PixelLab map-object.

Game: nearest-filtered; muzzle = additive HUD quad at barrel (anim_t flicker in shader); blood = world billboard at hit; shells = alpha HUD quads parented to weapon motion.
